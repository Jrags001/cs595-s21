Parks & Recreation

 

Parks & Recreation is one of my favorite television shows of all time. The first season was very similar to "The Office", however from Season 2 until the end of the series, it became something entirely of its own, and in my opinion, had supreme quality all the way until the end.

One fun fact is that during the the final season, the show had jumped three years into the future from 2014, and correctly predicted that the Cubs won the 2016 World Series.
