Metallica

 

Metallica is my favorite band of all time. I discovered them when I was 10, and haven't stopped listening since. I have seen them five times, with the most recent time at AT&T Stadium in Dallas, TX. Fear not, I was in Snake Pit and was mere feet away from the band.

They were my first ever concert in 1998 at the Virginia Beach Ampitheater. Someone was kind enough to put the full bootleg of the concert on YouTube.
